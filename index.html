About Me

I am a third year Engineering Physics student who enjoys working with robots, embedded systems, and aerospace related projects. I have been competing in VEX robotics since grade 10, and am currently involved in Penguin Robotics for robotics mentoring. I also served as co-captain of UBC RoboMaster, a competitive autonomous robotics team founded by me and my friends. Aside from school and student teams, I also enjoy playing musical instruments, caligraphy, and journaling.
